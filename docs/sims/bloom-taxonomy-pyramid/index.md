## About This MicroSim

An interactive pyramid divided into six horizontal tiers representing the levels of Bloom's Taxonomy, from Remember at the base to Create at the top. Each tier uses a warm blue-to-orange gradient.

In **Explore mode**, hover over any tier to see a tooltip with the level name, one-sentence definition, example verbs, and a sample learning objective written at that level.

In **Quiz mode**, a randomly chosen learning objective appears at the top. Click the tier that matches its Bloom level. Correct answers highlight green; incorrect answers reveal the correct tier with an explanation.

**Learning Objective (Apply):** Given a learning objective sentence, identify the Bloom level it targets.
