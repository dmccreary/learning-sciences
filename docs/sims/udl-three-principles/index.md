## About This MicroSim

An interactive display of the three Universal Design for Learning (UDL) principles: **Engagement** (the Why), **Representation** (the What), and **Action & Expression** (the How). Each pillar shows three levels from the UDL Guidelines: Provide Options, Build, and Internalize.

**Explore Mode:** Hover over any pillar section to see the UDL checkpoint name, a plain-English description, and two intelligent-textbook examples.

**Quiz Mode:** An intelligent-textbook feature is described. Click the pillar and section that the feature most directly serves.

**Learning Objective** (Bloom level: Apply): *Given an intelligent-textbook feature, identify which UDL principle and checkpoint it most directly serves.*
