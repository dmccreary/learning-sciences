## About This MicroSim

A gallery of the seven Bloom the Elephant mascot poses: Neutral, Welcome, Thinking, Tip, Warning, Encouraging, and Celebration. Each pose is displayed in a color-coded cell matching its admonition type.

**Explore Mode:** Hover over any pose to see the admonition type it pairs with, a concrete learning moment where it fits best, and the reason (in SDT or cognitive-load terms) it is the right pose for that moment.

**Quiz Mode:** A short scenario describes a learning moment. Click the pose that best fits. Correct answers are confirmed with an explanation.

**Learning Objective** (Bloom level: Apply): *Given a learning moment in a chapter, select the mascot pose whose pedagogical function fits best.*
