## About This MicroSim

An interactive rubric board with 20 criteria grouped into five color-coded categories: **Structure** (learning graph, outlines, glossary, navigation), **Seven Domains** (motivation, understanding, retention, application), **Engagement** (MicroSims, mascot, stories, quizzes), **Measurement** (Bloom coverage, metrics, dashboard, retrieval), and **Systems & Evidence** (causal loops, critical thinking, references, writing style).

**Explore Mode:** Hover over any rubric item to see its full criterion text, the automated tool that checks it (if any), and examples of what passing and failing look like.

**Self-Audit Mode:** Click items to cycle through scores (Fail, Partial, Pass). A summary bar reports counts for each score level.

**Learning Objective** (Bloom level: Evaluate): *Given a chapter draft, judge its readiness to ship by applying the 20-item rubric and identifying the items most likely to fail.*
