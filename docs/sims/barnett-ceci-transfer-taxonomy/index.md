## About This MicroSim

This interactive infographic shows a hexagonal radar diagram with six Barnett and Ceci dimensions of transfer distance: Knowledge Domain, Physical Context, Temporal Context, Functional Context, Social Context, and Modality. Each axis extends from "near" at the center to "far" at the outer edge.

**Modes:**

- **Explore** -- hover over any axis to see the dimension definition, near and far examples, and a note about common designer blind spots
- **Quiz** -- read a training-to-transfer scenario and click near/far on each axis, then press Enter to check your answers

**Learning Objective (Analyze):** Given a training context and a transfer task, identify which Barnett and Ceci dimensions of distance the transfer crosses and rate each as near or far.
