## About This MicroSim

A seven-segment pie wheel showing the domains of learning sciences in the forward-chain order: Motivation, Understanding, Retention, Application, Expertise, Measurement, and Learning Conditions. Learning Conditions has a dashed border to signal its substrate role.

In **Explore mode**, hover over any segment to see its definition, example design decisions, and chapter link.

In **Diagnose mode**, read a chapter excerpt and click the domain(s) it serves. Double-click to submit your answer. Correct selections turn green; missed domains turn amber.

**Learning Objective (Analyze):** Given a piece of instruction, identify which domains it serves well and which it under-serves.
