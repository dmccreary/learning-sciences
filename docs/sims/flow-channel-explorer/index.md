## About This MicroSim

An interactive plot with Skill on the x-axis and Challenge on the y-axis. The diagonal band represents the flow channel, where challenge and skill are well-matched. Above the diagonal lies the anxiety zone (and beyond it, panic). Below the diagonal lies the boredom zone (and beyond it, apathy).

Use the **Challenge** and **Skill** sliders to move the learner marker and watch which zone they land in. Enable **Show trajectory** to see a fading trail as you adjust the sliders -- useful for simulating a learner whose skill grows during a session.

**Learning Objective (Apply):** Given a learner profile, adjust task challenge so that the learner lands in the flow channel rather than anxiety or boredom.
