## About This MicroSim

This MicroSim implements a six-stage fading progression for a chosen concept. Starting from a fully worked example, each stage removes one more step, until the learner solves independently. A cognitive load meter shows extraneous load decreasing as the learner takes on more responsibility.

**Controls:**

- **Concept** -- choose from Bayes' Rule or Conditional Probability
- **Previous/Next Stage** -- step through the six fading stages
- **Self-Explain Prompts** -- toggle "why does this step work?" prompts
- **Reset** -- return to stage 1

**Learning Objective (Apply):** Given a target concept and a learner profile, choose the appropriate stage in the fading progression and justify the choice in cognitive-load terms.
